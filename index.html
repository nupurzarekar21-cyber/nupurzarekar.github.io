I'm Nupur Zarekar, a senior account executive currently at Sprouts Edu Tours, where I manage receivables, expense reporting, and reconciliation across 200+ school tours a year.

What sets my work apart is what I did when the manual process stopped scaling: instead of asking for more headcount, I taught myself to build with AI development tools and shipped a custom internal ERP — now the operational backbone for finance and operations across all three of the company's branches.

I'm a B.Com graduate (CGPA 8.49, A+) from R.A. Podar College, with a Financial Markets certification from Yale via Coursera.
